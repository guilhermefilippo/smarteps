Technical Features

Learn about all the technical advantages of the Smart EPS system

Coating Application

The coating can be applied directly to the Smart EPS Plug'n'Play Shaft. We recommend ready-to-use mortar or additive to incorporate into adhesive mortar.

Lightweight

EPS is extremely light, facilitating transport and handling at the construction site. Enormous weight reduction compared to conventional methods in foundation and structure.

Less Waste Generation

The use of EPS shafts does not generate waste, eliminating the need for dumpsters and associated costs.

Sustentabilidade

EPS is recyclable and its application contributes to more sustainable construction practices. Smart EPS shaft components are recyclable, including reinforcement plates.

Moisture Resistance

Unlike the traditional method, EPS is waterproof, allowing shaft installation even in rainy conditions without compromising quality.

Clean Construction

Smart EPS shaft installation is clean, eliminating dust and debris, contributing to a safer and more organized work environment.

Flame-Retardant EPS

Meets civil construction standards, ensuring building safety with flame-retardant properties.

Environmental Certifications

Companies that adopt recyclable materials can improve their corporate image and meet environmental certification criteria.

Quick Fit

All projects are designed to fit perfectly to speed up the installation process.
